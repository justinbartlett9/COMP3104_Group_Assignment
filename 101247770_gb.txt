George Brown College is a public college of applied arts and technology that is located in Toronto, 
Ontario, Canada. They currently have three campuses all within the city of Toronto. These campuses 
are Casa Loma, St. James, and Waterfront. They offer diplomas, advanced diplomas and eight-degree 
programs for attending students. At the time of writing, Geroge Brown houses approximately 26,000 
full-time students between their campuses, 3,500 internation students, 3,700 part-time students and 
62,800 continuing education students. George Brown has many students who find work after graduation, 
and prides themselves on thsi statistic. They also were named Greater Toronto's Top Employer in 2012 
based on the morale of their staff and progressive working conditions for their staff.
